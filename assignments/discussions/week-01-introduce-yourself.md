# Discussion: Introduce Yourself!

**Type:** Graded Discussion  
**Module:** 1 — Getting Started  
**Points:** See Canvas

## Prompt

Welcome! We are all new to this class so please take a moment to introduce yourself to your classmates. Not sure what to say? Start with:

- **Name**
- Do you work outside of being a student? If so, let us know where and what you do there.
- Do you live outside of Cincinnati? If so, where?
- What is your experience with Python (newbie, beginner, intermediate)?
- What experience do you have with other data science programming languages (i.e. R, SQL, SAS)?
